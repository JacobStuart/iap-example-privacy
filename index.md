Last updated: 2026-04-08
This Privacy Policy explains how Jacob’s Test Org (“we”, “us”, “our”) handles information when you use the IAP-Example application (the “App”) on Meta Quest / Meta Horizon platform services.
1) What this policy covers
This policy covers information processed when you:
launch and use the App, and/or
purchase or restore in-app purchases (add-ons / downloadable content) associated with the App.
2) Information we access or receive
Depending on your usage and platform availability, we may access the following categories of information via Meta platform features:
A. User ID
User ID / platform identifier associated with your Meta/Quest account, used to identify the user for app functionality and entitlement checks.
B. User profile (limited)
Limited profile information made available by the platform (for example, a display name/username or profile-related fields), if the App requests and is granted access to such fields.
C. In-app purchase information
Purchase/entitlement status for add-ons or downloadable content (e.g., whether you own a given SKU such as premium_unlock), used to unlock content or features.
Transaction/purchase metadata returned by the platform as part of the purchase flow (e.g., purchase tokens/receipt identifiers), if provided.

We use the information above to:
Provide core app functionality tied to the user (e.g., associating app state or unlocks with a user).
Process, validate, and restore purchases and to grant access to purchased add-ons / downloadable content.
Maintain security, prevent abuse/fraud, and troubleshoot issues (e.g., debugging failed purchase flows).

We do not sell personal information.
We do not share personal information with third parties except:
Service providers (if any) that help us operate the App, under obligations to protect the information and only process it for our instructions; and/or
When required to comply with law, legal process, or enforce our rights.

We retain information only as long as reasonably necessary to:
operate the App,
